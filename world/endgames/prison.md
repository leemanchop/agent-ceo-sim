# Endgames — Prison

See `../schemas.md` for record format.

The fraud_score / fbi_awareness corner of the matrix. Length scales with severity: 25y federal at the top, a 6-month plea at the bottom, with the SBF/Holmes/Atherton-house-arrest tiers between. The post-mortem long read uses the John Carreyrou register — sourced, footnoted, structured around "the moment everything went wrong." Share card is the mugshot trading card: jumpsuit color, peak valuation, sentence length, one-liner.
