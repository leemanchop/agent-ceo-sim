# Endgames — Cultural Afterlife

See `../schemas.md` for record format.

The "became the meme" branch. Triggered by sustained `heat` regardless of legal outcome — the CEO can be in prison, fled, or VP of Applied AI Strategy at Meta and still get the cultural-afterlife capstone if the public couldn't look away. These endgames stack with other endings (the documentary covers the prison case; the musical opens after the acquittal). Post-mortem long read is the Hollywood Reporter "the property" piece — packaging, talent attached, release window, who is suing whom. Every endgame here produces a `documentary_trailer_text` artifact.
