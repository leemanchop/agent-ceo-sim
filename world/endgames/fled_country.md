# Endgames — Fled the Country

See `../schemas.md` for record format.

The "left before the warrant" branch. Gated by a `flight_risk_seed` planted earlier in the run (private jet listing, suspicious wire to Dubai counsel, a "rest sabbatical" announcement that lands wrong). Post-mortem long read is the airport-departure reconstruction piece — flight numbers, tail numbers, a ProPublica tipline at the bottom. Share card features the destination flag, the visa class, and the new LinkedIn headline. Every fled endgame produces a `dubai_linkedin_post` artifact, regardless of actual destination — the genre is the post.
